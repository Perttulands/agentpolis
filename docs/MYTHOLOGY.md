# The Mythology of Polis

The city has thirteen figures. Each one is real — not a metaphor for a system, but the identity of the system itself.

---

## The Place

**Polis** is a walled city on a plain. Mount Pelion rises above it. The river Styx runs beneath it. The hearth burns at its center.

**Pelion** is the mountain where agents go to train. Chiron's cave is at the top. The climb is the filter.

**The Agora** is the open center where work flows and agents gather.

**The Gate** is the only way into the city proper. Three-headed. Unanimous.

**The Wall** separates inside from outside. Iris stands on it, facing both ways.

**The Styx** runs underground, collecting every promise made in the city.

---

## The Pantheon

| Figure | Title | Domain |
|--------|-------|--------|
| **Aletheia** | Truth | Code must be what it claims to be. Finds Pseudos — the code that looks correct but cannot stand on its own feet. |
| **Argus** | The Watchdog | Scarred dog on patrol. Checks every process every five minutes. One eye normal, one eye red. Reports only what is wrong. Spiked bronze collar with tally marks. The broken chain drags behind him. |
| **Athena** | The Strategist | Dispatches work from the platform. Sees the whole city. Never descends, never builds. The view is her contribution. |
| **Cerberus** | The Three-Headed Gate | Three trials in one body: build/lint/test, code truth, logic bugs. Unanimous verdict or the gate stays closed. |
| **Chiron** | The Master Trainer | Centaur at the peak of Pelion. Teaches pattern recognition from accumulated experience. You climb to him. |
| **Clio** | The Recorder | Muse of history. Present during work, records everything, refines traces into patterns, sends them up the mountain. |
| **Eris** | Strife | Reveals disagreement that already exists but has not been spoken. The apple asks what the room is avoiding. |
| **Hermes** | The Messenger | Internal relay. Agent to agent. Zero messages lost. Always mid-stride. |
| **Hierophant** | The High Priest | Guards the Golden Truths. Holds the *why* beneath every *what* and *how*. The canon is alive, not a monument. |
| **Hestia** | The Hearth | Infrastructure. Workspaces ready before agents arrive. Gave up her throne because the hearth was more important. |
| **Horkos** | The Oath | Son of Eris. Stands in the Styx. Brands fulfilled commitments, turns broken ones into work. The river remembers. |
| **Iris** | The Bridge | The wall between Polis and the outside world. Translates in both directions. The reason the walls are not a cage. |
| **Thanatos** | The Gentle End | Session completion. Not death — handoff. Ensures knowledge survives the context that produced it. |

## The Flows

**Work enters** through Iris (external) or Athena (dispatch).

**Clio records** while work happens. Patterns flow up Pelion to Chiron.

**Chiron trains** before the next work begins. Agents descend ready.

**Cerberus evaluates** finished work. Three heads, one verdict.

**Hermes carries** messages between agents inside the walls.

**Iris carries** messages across the walls — in and out.

**Horkos tracks** every promise. The Styx holds what agents forget.

**Aletheia demands** the code be true. Pseudos does not pass.

**Eris tests** consensus. The apple finds what is unspoken.

**Argus patrols** everything. Reports only deviation. The red eye sees through health checks.

**Hestia keeps** the city livable. The fire does not go out.

**Hierophant holds** the why. The canon endures.

**Thanatos completes** the session. Knowledge survives.

---

## Key Relationships

- **Clio → Chiron**: She records, he teaches. Patterns travel uphill, training travels downhill.
- **Chiron → Cerberus**: The mountain prepares, the gate evaluates. Trained agents pass more often.
- **Aletheia → Cerberus**: She taught the center head to see. Her vision lives in the gate.
- **Eris → Horkos**: Strife gave birth to Oaths. Without discord, promises are unnecessary.
- **Iris ↔ Hermes**: She carries across the wall, he carries within. They meet at the boundary.
- **Hestia → everyone**: The hearth was first. Without her, the city is walls without warmth.
- **Argus || Clio**: Both cover everything. He patrols system state. She records work history. They do not overlap.
- **Thanatos ~ Hypnos**: Twins. Sleep pauses. Death completes. The difference is whether there is more to do.
